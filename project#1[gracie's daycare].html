Starlight Nightcare: Where we offer a happy and free daycare under the stars!

Introduction

Hi! I'm Gracie Smith, the owner/founder of Starlight Nightcare, where we take care of infants/children with parents who have the night shift or working late. We understand that many of us have busy workdays, and even busier nights. We get that many works overtime or nightshift to provide for young ones, and not always round to be there for nightime, Starlight Nightcare can solve that.


What we offer

  • Overnight supervision 24/7 by trained caregivers and nurses
  • Evening activities (calm play, storytime, TV/movie time) and bedtime routines at apporiate hours
  • Cosy and safe sleeping/nursery areas for your child's sleep[with fun night themes/a glow-in-the-dark solor system to fit the mood]
  • Flexible drop-off and pick-up times
  • Light snacks and comfort care[or wetties] during the night

Hours

We are open from Mondays - Sundays at early evening to morning to parents working hours, parents are free to pick up kids before the official endtime no catch!

  • Monday to Thursday: 6:00 PM – 7:00 AM
  • Friday to Sunday: 6:00 PM – 8:00 AM
